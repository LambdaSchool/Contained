What is controller containment and what does it offer developers?
View controller containment is when one view controller places another view controller inside of it. Developers can use controller containment to break up the view into separate view controllers with their own logic to make code more modular and more organized.

What is the difference between navigation and tab controllers? Under what circumstances do you use each one?
Use a navigation controller when moving through related screens of information. Use a tab bar to organize information at the app level. A tab bar is a good way to flatten your information hierarchy and provide access to several peer information categories or modes at once.
